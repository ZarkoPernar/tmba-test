Short Guide to Transactions and gas fees

  • Where do I get ETH?

    ETH stands for Ether, the native cryptocurrency of the Ethereum blockchain. You can buy ETH on cryptocurrency exchanges like Coinbase, Gemini or Kraken

  • What is the gas fee?

    Think of gas as a variable transaction fee paid directly to the Ethereum network to process each order. The Token MBA team does not set or receive this fee.

  • How much is the gas fee?

    It depends. Gas fees increase when there are many concurrent users trying to transact at the same time. Ethereum wallets automatically estimate the gas fee for you.

  • Why is my transaction pending?

    Transactions must be broadcast to the Ethereum network and then confirmed before a purchase is successful. Sometimes this can take several minutes if the network is congested

  • How do I view my NFT?

    Once your transaction is successful, you can view your token(s) on the “My Token” page. You can also view your tokens by connecting your Ethereum wallet to NFT marketplaces like www.opensea.io

Transacting on the blockchain is a little different. Here’s what you’ll need.

 

We only accept payment in Ether (ETH), the native cryptocurrency of the Ethereum blockchain. We think the best way to learn about a new technology is to use it!

If you haven’t purchased cryptocurrencies or NFTs before, it can be a bit daunting but we’ll walk you through the process below.

Step 1: Purchase ETH from a cryptocurrency exchange like Coinbase, Gemini or Kraken

Step 2: Create your own Ethereum wallet. We recommend MetaMask (https://metamask.io/) if you are new to Ethereum.

Chrome, Firefox, Brave and Edge web browsers all support MetaMask. You can also download MetaMask on the iOS and Android app stores but we recommend a desktop browser if this is new to you.

Step 3: Transfer ETH from your Coinbase/Gemini/Kraken account to your new Ethereum wallet address. You will find your MetaMask address under account name (in the format 0x12r45...6HJ9). Copy and paste this address to receive funds, never try and type it out manually.

Step 4: You should now have ETH in your MetaMask wallet. Navigate to www.tokenmba.io on your desktop browser, click on the “Connect Wallet” button and proceed with your purchase.
